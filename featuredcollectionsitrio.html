Adewole Yetunde-Creative Director

Designers Spotlight

I started my fashion career in 2007.I learnt it for 3 months.After completion in the same
year, I started sewing in my fathers sitting room, and was working as a stylist with a sister of mine.I went back for my degree, and I started sewing for my mates at school.

So whenever I'm on semester break, I am at my business.The journey wasn't easy but the struggle continued.
I started with a machine, later, had one apprentice, which led me to move to an office apartment in 2009 and since then being building myself.

I started with ankara sewing and then was challenged into sewing all types of female ready-to-wear clothing.
